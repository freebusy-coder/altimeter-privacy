Altimeter - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Altimeter ("the App") is developed by Jackson Chen. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data remains on your device only.

Location Data

The App accesses your device's GPS location solely to calculate and display altitude. This location data is used in real-time and is never stored, uploaded, or shared with any third party.

Internet Access

The App uses internet access only to load map tiles from OpenStreetMap when you view your location on the map. No personal data is transmitted.

Third Party Services

The App uses OpenStreetMap for map display. OpenStreetMap's privacy policy can be found at https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
